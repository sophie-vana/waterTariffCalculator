You can easily make payments online through the official City of Cape Town website. Simply log in to your account and securely pay your bills using your credit or debit card. Alternatively, many local banks offer bill payment services through their online banking platforms or at their physical branches. For those who prefer face-to-face transactions, designated payment centers across the city provide a convenient option to settle bills in cash or electronically.
